Fanzine

Creation of a FANZINE in collaboration with REGADOR, where we explored the work of some of the most influential designers of modern times through graphic compositions. The fanzine was produced using silk printing techniques, which added a tactile, handcrafted quality to the final result. The featured designer was Paula Scher, whose iconic style inspired much of the visual direction. A key challenge in this project was working within the constraint of using only two colors, in addition to black and white, which pushed us to focus on creative composition and effective color application to convey impactful designs.
