Somnium

The Somnium project was a collaborative project with Vogue Portugal and master's students from IADE. Our goal was to create a brand identity and product design for Somnium, a shower gel that embodies tranquility. As a unique promotional item, it would be included as a gift with Vogue Portugal magazine purchases.

We designed the Somnium logo and branding elements to reflect the dreamlike, surreal world, inspiring the shower gel’s soft bubble/cloud shape. We 3D-printed the final product, which was included in a special kit featuring the magazine, the shower gel, a sleep mask, and a calming body oil, enhancing the experience of relaxation before bedtime.
